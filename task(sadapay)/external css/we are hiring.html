Our Mission:

To make money so simple (“sada”) that any other way becomes unthinkable.

Banking in Pakistan is notoriously complex and intimidating, and because of this, more than 80% of 225 million people don’t have a bank account.

We’re built on the idea that if we eliminate all of this unnecessary complexity and focus only on what really matters, we can deliver a banking experience that is so elegant and simple that absolutely anyone can use it. 

Better access to modern financial services will have a far-reaching impact that touches every industry and every part of our lives. We are a relentless group of individuals, who genuinely believe they can build a financially happier Pakistan. Our story is one of empowerment and inclusivity for over a hundred million people, and we want you to be a part of it!
